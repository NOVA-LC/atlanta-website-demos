## Truthful answer: I found ~4 actionable leads, not 10.

This isn't because the searches were lazy — it's because the public web is structurally a poor source for this exact kind of lead. Reasons:

- **Reddit and Upwork** are blocked by safety policies and Cloudflare
- **LinkedIn** post search isn't accessible without auth
- **Facebook groups** (where most "anyone know a web designer?" posts actually live) require membership

- **Search engine indexing lags 3-7 days** behind fresh posts
